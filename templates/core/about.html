Our Mission

DIU BZSF is a dedicated community organization built on the foundation of solidarity, mutual support, and academic excellence. We bring together students from Bhola Zila studying at Daffodil International University to create a unified, empowering environment where every member can thrive.

🔭

Our Vision

We envision a future where DIU BZSF becomes the most impactful student community from Bhola — one that nurtures leadership, fosters innovation, and creates lasting bonds between its members. We aim to leave a meaningful legacy both at DIU and in our home district of Bhola.

📜

Our History

Founded by a passionate group of Bhola students at Daffodil International University, BZSF has grown steadily over the years. What began as informal gatherings evolved into a structured organization with clear goals, defined leadership, and a growing membership base. Through events, cultural programs, and collective action, BZSF has become a cornerstone of campus life for students from Bhola.

🏛️

Leadership Structure

Led by an elected President, Vice President, General Secretary, and Press Secretary, DIU BZSF operates with transparency and accountability. Our leadership team works tirelessly to represent member interests, organize events, and drive the organization forward every semester.
